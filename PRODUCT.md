# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Art directors, creative studios, and potential clients seeking a graphic and motion designer for branding, motion design, and visual identity projects. They visit to assess craft and personality before reaching out.

## Product Purpose
Portfolio website to showcase graphic design and motion design work, establish a distinctive creative identity, and generate client or job inquiries. Success: a visitor says "I want to work with her."

## Brand Personality
Experimental, playful, free. Bold compositions that surprise without overwhelming. The work speaks louder than the description.

## Anti-references
- Behance-style generic template: equal 3-column grids, white backgrounds, no visual personality.
- Awwwards-extreme: scroll-hijacking, 10 competing typefaces, experimental interactions that overshadow the work.
- Predictable portfolio templates: feature-card grids, predictable navigation, zero visual risk.

## Design Principles
1. The work is the hero. Layout and motion frame the work; they never compete with it.
2. Surprise in structure, discipline in execution. Unexpected compositions with clean code and precise detail.
3. One committed move beats ten safe ones. Bold choices held consistently over timid variety.
4. Personality in restraint. Playful does not mean loud; it means unexpected choices applied with precision.
5. Motion earns its place. Every animation communicates hierarchy, sequence, or state. Nothing moves decoratively.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion respected for all transitions. High contrast on dark background throughout.
